# Development-To-Lease-Up Continuity Strategy v1

Branch: `docs/strategic-execution-plan-enterprise-readiness-v1`
Scope: strategic future module planning only; no implementation.

## Strategic Frame

This is not construction management.

RentChain should not attempt to replace construction management software. The opportunity is narrower:

```text
development/project readiness -> lease-up -> operations handoff
```

The value is continuity from development readiness into leasing, occupancy, operations, maintenance, and audit.

## Customer Idea Captured

Enterprise feedback included a development and architecture context. The idea was not only rental operations after occupancy. It included the operational handoff from development readiness into lease-up.

Potential future inputs:

- permits/checklists
- contractors
- purchase orders
- material costs
- occupancy readiness
- unit activation
- vacancy publishing
- applications
- screening
- lease
- operations
- maintenance
- audit

## Future Enterprise/Development Module

This should be treated as a future Enterprise or Development module.

Potential scope:

- project or building readiness checklist
- unit activation readiness
- occupancy permit milestone tracking
- handoff checklist from development to leasing
- public availability preparation
- application pipeline launch
- screening and lease workflow linkage
- operational readiness audit

## Boundaries

The module should not include:

- full construction project management
- construction accounting
- subcontractor payment management
- material procurement execution
- live permit submission
- replacement of construction management platforms
- autonomous compliance determination

## Continuity Model

Recommended future lifecycle:

```text
Project readiness
  -> building readiness
  -> unit readiness
  -> vacancy publishing readiness
  -> applications
  -> screening
  -> lease execution
  -> occupancy
  -> tenant portal
  -> operations and maintenance
  -> evidence and audit history
```

## Governance Requirements

Any future module must preserve:

- landlord ownership of property and unit data
- staff and PM company actor attribution
- contractor boundaries
- projection-safe public listing data
- tenant privacy
- audit history
- manual review for compliance-sensitive decisions

## Why It Matters

Large landlords and developers often lose continuity between development completion and lease-up execution.

RentChain can differentiate by connecting:

- readiness
- availability
- applications
- screening
- lease execution
- operations

without trying to become a full construction management system.
