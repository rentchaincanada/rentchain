Next Mission: feat/property-manager-company-acceptance-api-v1

Backend/API-only acceptance endpoint for pending landlord-to-property-manager-company relationships. This completes the mutual-acceptance lifecycle by allowing an authorized PM Company Owner or Admin to move a landlord-created pending relationship to active, without adding UI, email, dashboards, billing delegation, or staff assignment workflows.

Mission Goal

Implement the company-side acceptance API for pending landlord-company relationships. The API must require an authenticated active Company Owner/Admin membership for the target PM company, validate that the relationship belongs to that company and is still pending, transition it to active, and append a metadata-only audit event.

Accepted Lifecycle

Step Actor State
Relationship creation Landlord Owner pending; no company authority is granted.
Relationship acceptance PM Company Owner/Admin pending -> active; company consent is recorded.
Future authority evaluation Company staff Active relationship can later support scoped authority through membership, role, and scope checks.

Required API

Proposed route: POST /api/property-manager-companies/:companyId/relationships/:relationshipId/accept. The route is company-scoped and must not rely on landlord identifiers supplied in the query or body.

Acceptance Contract

  • Authenticated user is required.
  • User must have active PM company membership for :companyId.
  • User role must be company_owner or company_admin.
  • Company acceptance is performed only on behalf of the user's own PM company.
  • Relationship must belong to the same PM company.
  • Relationship status must be pending.
  • Accepted relationship records preserve landlord ownership and approved scope.
  • Acceptance cannot transfer relationships, alter company ownership, or modify landlord-approved scope.
  • Pending relationship does not grant access before acceptance.
  • Active relationship does not grant billing/settings authority.

Own-Company Governance Rule

A PM Company Owner/Admin may accept only for their own company. They may not activate another company's relationship, transfer the relationship, change company ownership, or negotiate property/workspace scope during acceptance. Acceptance means agree to the landlord-approved relationship, not alter the relationship.

Audit Contract

Acceptance must append a metadata-only event:

landlord_company_relationship_activated relationship_activated pending -> active

Metadata must include actor user, actor company, PM company, landlord context, relationship, status transition, property and workspace scope, outcome, timestamp, and target resource. Audit payloads must not contain secrets, provider payloads, or user-facing raw ID labels.

Security Model

  • Landlord owners cannot activate relationships through this route.
  • Non-admin company staff cannot accept relationships.
  • Inactive, suspended, removed, malformed, or mismatched memberships fail closed.
  • Relationships for a different PM company fail closed.
  • Active, suspended, or terminated relationships cannot be accepted.
  • Malformed relationship status or scope fails closed.
  • No cross-landlord data is exposed beyond the accepted relationship projection.
  • Route company ID must match both active membership and relationship company ownership.
  • Landlord scope, property scope, and workspace scope cannot be overridden from body or query parameters.
  • Billing/settings remains landlord-owner only.
  • No contractor organization access or staff assignment authority is introduced.

Non-Goals

No UI, company dashboard, invite emails, contractor organizations, billing delegation, custom permissions, staff assignment UI, frontend routes, production data migration, or backfill work.
