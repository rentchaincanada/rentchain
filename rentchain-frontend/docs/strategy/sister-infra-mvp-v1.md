## Positioning (One Page)

Sister Infrastructure is a separate infrastructure product narrative focused on portable trust and workflow interoperability for rental participants. It is not a consumer token product and not a blockchain marketing program. The goal is to deliver operational value to operators and partners through verifiable records, consent-aware data exchange, and audit-ready lifecycle artifacts.

This initiative must remain distinct from the RentChain SaaS product narrative:

- RentChain SaaS: landlord workflow product (applications, screening, leases, payments, operations).
- Sister Infrastructure: infrastructure layer narrative for partner interoperability and reusable identity/record primitives.

## MVP Scope: Tenant Passport

Tenant Passport MVP includes:

- Portable tenant profile package with explicit consent boundaries.
- Core attestations: identity checks, screening completion state, payment behavior summary, and lease lifecycle references.
- Partner-share package format with minimal required fields.
- Audit metadata: issuedAt, source system, version, and expiration guidance.
- Revocation/update handling at the record-set level.

## Out of Scope (MVP)

- No token issuance.
- No blockchain marketing language.
- No speculative investment narrative.
- No public exchange or wallet-first experience.
- No legal decision automation replacing user/partner review.

## Revenue Model Options

1. B2B API access licensing for partner platforms.
2. Verification package generation fees (per package or volume tier).
3. Enterprise compliance and audit export add-on.
4. White-label integration package with implementation support.
5. Tiered developer plan for sandbox-to-production onboarding.

## Success Criteria

- Partner reviewers can understand boundary and compliance posture quickly.
- Narrative is operational and compliance-oriented.
- Naming and architecture reduce confusion with existing SaaS brand and domains.
