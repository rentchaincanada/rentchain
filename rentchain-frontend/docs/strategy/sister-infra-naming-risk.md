# Sister Infrastructure Naming Risk

Related docs:
- [Sister Infrastructure MVP v1](./sister-infra-mvp-v1.md)
- [Architecture Boundary](./sister-infra-architecture-boundary.md)

## Objective

Define naming guardrails that avoid confusion with `rentchain.io` and keep the infrastructure product clearly separate from the SaaS brand.

## Trademark Checklist

- Run preliminary trademark screening in core markets before brand lock.
- Check class overlap for software, data exchange, and identity verification.
- Validate conflict risk for phonetically similar names.
- Confirm social handle availability for shortlisted names.
- Perform legal review before public launch.

## Domain Strategy

- Use a distinct root domain that does not look like a typo or variant of `rentchain.io`.
- Avoid domain names containing `rentchain` + minor suffix that imply same product line.
- Keep public docs and trust policies on the distinct domain.
- Use explicit cross-site disclaimers when linking between SaaS and infrastructure sites.

## Explicit Confusion Avoidance Rules

- Do not use `rentchain.io` variants for infrastructure branding.
- Do not reuse SaaS-only product names for infrastructure endpoints.
- Do not present infrastructure docs as sub-features of SaaS pricing pages.
- Add a fixed disclosure line where relevant: "This infrastructure offering is separate from the RentChain SaaS workflow product."

## Decision Gate

Before external launch, naming must pass:

- legal check
- domain check
- brand confusion review
- partner readability review
